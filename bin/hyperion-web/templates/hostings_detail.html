Operator transfer cheatsheet

  1. Click Export above on the source node.
  2. On your workstation: scp -r root@source:/var/lib/hyperion/migration/<bundle_id> root@target:/var/lib/hyperion/migration/
  3. On the target node: sudo hctl hosting import --manifest /var/lib/hyperion/migration/<bundle_id>/manifest.json
  4. Update DNS to point at the target's IP. The importer logs say "click Issue Cert" when LE-ready.
  5. Once the migration is verified live: hctl hosting delete <domain> on the source.

Danger zone

Deleting removes the hosting's site, files, databases, and Linux user. Suspending serves a 503 page, stops PHP-FPM, locks the DB user, kills running processes — fully reversible.
